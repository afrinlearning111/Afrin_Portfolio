01 About

A QA engineer who thinks like a developer.

I design test strategies that catch defects early and automation frameworks that scale with the product — written, reviewed, and maintained like production software.

Over the past four years, I've shipped critical software in regulated domains where reliability isn't optional. From validating EHR communication and insurance eligibility flows in healthcare, to securing payment gateway transactions in FinTech, to verifying real-time IoT telemetry over Kafka streams in automotive — I've learned that quality is a discipline, not a phase.

I take ownership of the test pyramid end-to-end: writing maintainable Selenium and Appium frameworks in Java, integrating them into Azure DevOps pipelines, validating APIs with REST Assured and Postman, and mentoring junior QAs along the way. Lately I've been exploring how prompt engineering and AI tooling can accelerate test data generation without sacrificing edge-case rigor.
